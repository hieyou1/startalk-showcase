Romance of the Three Kingdoms 三国演义

  • The book Romance of the Three Kingdoms is a text about the Chinese history that depicts the fights of powerful figures which leads to multiple dynasties rising and falling.
  • Some characters in the story that you may recognize are: Liu Bei, Zhang Fei, Guan Yu, Cao Cao, and Zhuge Liang.

Goals

  • Analyze the sentiment- Segmentation of Chinese words
  • Word frequency analysis: most common words in the book
  • Sentiment analysis
