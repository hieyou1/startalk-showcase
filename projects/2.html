白蛇

Once upon a time in the Mountain 峨眉, there were two snakes, 白蛇 and 黑蛇, both with magical power. 白蛇 changed into a very beautiful girl named 白素贞. 黑蛇 also became a lovely maid named 小青. They came to 西湖杭州 for a visit and were attracted by the beauty of the scene. There, over the 段家桥 came a young man named 许仙. 白素贞 immediately fell into love. To help her sister, 小青 set some tricks to let 许仙 and 白素贞 meet and finally get married.

When the three-attendee wedding ceremony was on, the Abbot, named 法海, of a remote Buddhist temple, 金山寺, sensed something and then found what was going on. He pledged to capture the 白蛇.

After the marriage, the three of them moved to 镇江市 on the 长江 where they set up a 草药店. 白素贞 wrote out the prescriptions while 许仙 and 小青 gathered and dispensed the herbal medicine. Patients unable to pay were given free treatment and medicine. The store quickly became well known and popular.

法海 found 许仙 there and warned him that his pregnant wife was a snake. He showed 许仙 a way to reveal his wife's true self--a festival alcohol drink to give to her during 端午节. On that day, 许仙 bought the festival alcohol with realgar to celebrate and asked 白素贞 to drink it with him. Under her husband's coaxing, 白素贞 could not find a reason to refuse the drink and she took a sip, thinking that her superior magic skills would make her immune to the power of realgar. But she immediately was stricken ill and barely managed to get to bed. 许仙 rushed to the bed and drew aside its curtain. 白素贞 was no loger there. In her place was a large 白蛇 coiled on the bed. So great was 许仙's shock that he fell to the floor and died.

When the power of realgar faded, 白素贞 returned to her human form. She was heartbroken to find 许仙 lying dead beside the bed. But she knew that 灵芝草, a celestial herb on 昆仑山, could restore him to life. She flew to 昆仑山 to steal the celestial herb but encountered the white crane and heavenly guards responsible for looking after the 灵芝草. They fought to prevent her from taking the herb and 白素贞 was losing the battle. Suddenly, a voice commanded them to stop. It was the voice of the Immortal of the Southern End. 白素贞 begged him in tears to help her. Impressed by her sincerity and perseverance, he granted her the 灵芝草, and the life of 许仙 was restored. He suddenly remembered what the Abbot said to him. Recovered from the illness, 许仙 went to the Temple. To separate him from his wife, 法海 forced him to become a monk. 白素贞, along with 小青, ran to 金山寺 and begged 法海 to let her husband go but got refused. Then in anger, she gathered together a great army of underwater creatures to attack the monastery and used water to submerge the temple. 法海 had a magic outer vestment, which made the mountain to grow so that the waterlevel was kept under the temple. 法海 also had the magic to command heavenly soldiers. 白素贞 was pregnant, so could not fight the battle any longer.

白素贞 and 小青 fled to 西湖杭州 to where they first met 许仙. 小青 was very angry at 许仙 for his unfaithfulness and pledged to kill him when she saw him. 许仙 was secretly released by a young monk when the two sides were fighting and happened to come to the same place. When they met him, 小青 wanted to attack him with her sword, but 白素贞 held her back and told 许仙 the truth. Now that they fully understood each other, they went home.

法海 went to the heaven and asked the heaven emperor to help him. A powerful fighter with a magic lantern was commanded to assist 法海. 白素贞 gave birth to a son. One day, 许仙 went to buy a hat for his son. The hat he bought turned out to be a lantern which made 白素贞 under the spell of the fighter. 白素贞 was then imprisoned under the 雷峰塔 by 西湖杭州. 黑蛇 fled to her original place and practiced her magic. Several years later, her magic was strong enough to take revenge. She managed to destroy the 雷峰塔 and rescued 白素贞, allowing 白素贞 to reunite with her husband and her son. 小青 or 黑蛇 then defeated 法海 and had him swallowed by a crab, and they lived happily ever after.
