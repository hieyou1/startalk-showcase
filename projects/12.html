Week 3: How to make Traditional Chinese Spring Rolls

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

  • Rice paper sheets(one for each spring Roll)
  • Thinly sliced cabbage
  • Thinly sliced carrots
  • Thinly sliced cucumbers
  • Long cooked noodles(cooled)
  • A bowl full of wate(big enough to fully submerge rice paper)

Step 2:Assemble your Spring Rolls

  1. Grab a piece of rice paper and submerge in the water for no longer than 5 seconds
  2. Lay your rice paer on a smooth flat surface being careful not to rip the paper
  3. Begin tp fill a small area in the center with your desired fillings, being careful not overfill it
  4. Fold two of the rice paper's sides tightly in towards the center, allowing them to overlap a little
  5. Roll the remaining Rice Paper over the filling until it forms a cylinder shape

Step 3:Plate and Enjoy!
