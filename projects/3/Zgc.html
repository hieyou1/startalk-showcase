中国菜谱：Chinese Dishes

Week 3: Cooking project: Dumplings and Spring Rolls Innovation Kitchen (AMU). In this week we learned about dumplings(饺子) and its relatively similar counter parts made by different ways of making them(EX: 煎饺，水饺，蒸饺，etc).

ingredieants for Spring Roll(春卷)

  • Noodles
  • Rice Paper
  • Cucumbers
  • Lettuce
  • Purple cabbage
  • Tomato

Steps to Make Spring Rolls(春卷)

  1. Dip rice paper into some water for 3-5 seconds.
  2. Add in the fillings of what you want on to the wet rice paper.
  3. Fold to hold the filling.
  4. Munch Time!
